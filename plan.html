Negotiation FAQ

Every objection, answered.

Use this section in the call. Open the relevant question, share the answer, move on. Built for honesty — not sales theatre.

What if my budget is below Spark ($1,500/mo)?＋

Lite is $300/mo with 1 dedicated specialist for ~2 hours/week — covers a single-page website, basic SEO, 1 social channel and monthly email. Or take a 2-week diagnostic sprint at $1,500 flat — audit, strategy, one quick win and a roadmap.

How are you cheaper without being lower quality?＋

India-based delivery means $20–$35/hr blended cost vs $75–$150/hr in NYC/SF; productized workflows + AI tooling make our hours more leveraged; and you keep paying market rate for the ad auction itself — only the agency layer is leaner.

Is website design really included at $300/mo?＋

Yes — every tier includes website design, scaled to the tier. Lite gets a one-page landing site; Spark gets 3–5 pages; Launch gets a 10+ page site with CRO landing pages; Growth and above get custom design systems and ongoing dev sprints.

Are daily uploads, SEO, email and social really in every tier?＋

Yes. Depth scales with the tier — Lite is 1 platform with light cadence, Enterprise is 24/7 production across every region. Every tier ships custom design assets, insights across all platforms, and an email program from day one.

How big is the team I get?＋

1 specialist on Lite, 2 on Spark, 4 on Launch, 6–10 on Growth, 10+ custom-staffed on Scale, and 12–50 embedded on Enterprise. Named individuals, not anonymous pooled hours.

Do you work with our existing tech stack?＋

Standard yes: GA4, GTM, Looker Studio, Segment, HubSpot, Salesforce, Klaviyo, Customer.io, Mixpanel, Amplitude, Shopify, WooCommerce, Webflow, WordPress, Framer. If you're on something niche, we'll integrate or recommend a swap with cost trade-offs documented.

What's the contract length? Who owns the accounts?＋

Month-to-month after a 90-day initial term; Enterprise can negotiate annual with quarterly reviews. You own every ad account, website, and asset — always. We get admin access; you get owner.
